In the not too near future humans will explore space. Likely, this will be not be done by humans in space sutis on rockets, but swarms of interconnected probes launched to the farthest reaches possible. Collecting data on these distant star-systems, stars, and planets will be almost fully automated.

Launch A Probe is a simulation of citizen science as we explore beyond our galaxy and into our celestial neighbors. This app was made using three.js.

To get started, create an account, then click the Explore Nearby Systems link. Scan a nearby star system to discover some basic information about its layout including the number of stars and planets. You can view details on those stars and planets, as well as rename them according to your own interests or naming scheme. You may also click the View Discovered Systems link to visit systems discovered by other explorers.
